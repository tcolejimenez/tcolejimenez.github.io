My Story

The path to programming started with realizing I could create something out of nothing in an instant. I was hooked.

My first programming language, Java, gave me a way to explore all the concepts I had floating around my head- I had been thinking in a modular or object-oriented way my whole life, I just didn't know it.

Next I stared exploring the web and all the magical things it can do, adding HTML, CSS and Javascript to my toolkit along the way.

Recognizing this is what I wanted to do for the rest of my life, I continued learning all could, including how to work from the server side with PHP and MySQL, how to make things even prettier with Bootstrap, and storing everything for safe keeping with Git.
